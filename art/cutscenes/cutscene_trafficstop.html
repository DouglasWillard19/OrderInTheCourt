A routine traffic stop.
Cruiser lights strobe across worn brick and busted sidewalks. One more stop on a long shift.
Camera & sound

Slow push in from behind the cruiser, lens flare off the lightbar. Street noise low in the mix: distant siren, bass from a passing car, wind against power lines.

Radio chatter bleeds in — half-heard codes, a dispatcher reading off plate info.
